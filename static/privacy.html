Privacy Policy

QBO Timesheet · Last updated: July 4, 2026

QBO Timesheet (the "Application") is a private, internal tool operated by Baykal Consulting solely for our own business. It is not offered to the public and has a single authorized user.

1. What the Application does

The Application connects to our own QuickBooks Online company through Intuit's APIs to create and read time-tracking entries (projects, clients, employees, service items, hours, and notes). The connection is established only after the QuickBooks account holder expressly authorizes it through Intuit's OAuth consent flow, and it operates in compliance with the Intuit Developer Terms of Service.

2. Data we access and store

  • QuickBooks data — read and written only as needed to display and record time entries. It remains in QuickBooks Online; the Application does not maintain a separate copy of it.
  • OAuth tokens — the credentials Intuit issues when we authorize the Application are stored encrypted-in-transit and kept on our own private server infrastructure, used only to communicate with Intuit's APIs.

3. What we do not do

  • We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose QuickBooks data to third parties for marketing or any other purpose. The only data transmission that occurs is with Intuit itself, as inherently required for API connectivity.
  • We do not use data for advertising, profiling, or analytics.
  • We do not collect data from anyone other than our own business.

4. Security

Access to the Application is password-protected and served exclusively over HTTPS. QuickBooks access can be revoked at any time from the QuickBooks "Apps" settings or by rotating credentials in the Intuit Developer portal.

5. Data retention and deletion

Stored tokens are deleted when the QuickBooks connection is revoked. Time entries live in QuickBooks Online and follow its retention rules.

6. Relationship to Intuit

The Application is independent and is not developed by, endorsed by, or affiliated with Intuit Inc. QuickBooks and QuickBooks Online are registered trademarks of Intuit Inc. Intuit's own handling of QuickBooks Online data is governed by Intuit's privacy statement.
